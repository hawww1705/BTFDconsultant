Accounting & Auditing Services

Providing independent verification, reporting accuracy, and corporate compliance strategies to safeguard your business assets.

Financial Statement & Internal Audits

Rigorous, independent assurance ensuring clarity, compliance, and corporate accountability.

An independent audit is critical to maintain investor trust and meet national banking requirements. Our team performs objective examinations of corporate financial data to confirm statement accuracy and framework alignment.

  • Financial Statement Audit: Formal examinations of balances, assets, and liabilities.
  • Internal Audit: Operational checks to review internal controls and protect company resources.

Accounting & Bookkeeping Services

Streamlined ledger management, accurate balancing, and strategic financial insights.

Managing day-to-day transactions demands precision. Our accounting division handles your journal entries, account reconciliations, cash-flow ledgers, and inventory valuations to keep data audit-ready.

  • Full-Cycle Bookkeeping: Systematic journal logs, receipts tracking, and billing records.
  • Monthly Reconciliations: Aligning internal bank, credit card, and vendor records.

Financial Reporting & IFRS Compliance

Comprehensive reports structured to global and national compliance benchmarks.

For multinational operations and companies preparing for public offerings, adherence to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) is mandatory. We verify and structure reports to ensure complete compatibility.

  • IFRS Alignment: Translating local ledgers to standard IFRS compliant accounts.
  • Financial Statements: Generation of Balance Sheets, Income Statements, and Cash Flow summaries.

Financial Risk Assessment

Spotting vulnerabilities, preparing stress tests, and engineering mitigation protocols.

Internal errors or external market fluctuations can jeopardize operations. Our risk advisory team evaluates your internal control framework to establish early-warning structures.

  • Internal Control Audit: Stress testing transactional authorizations to prevent leakage.
  • Vulnerability Identification: Finding cash-flow deficits or asset concentration concerns.

Our Audit Workflow

A meticulous, step-by-step engagement model ensuring thorough compliance and minimal operational downtime.

1

Planning & Scoping

We review your corporate footprint, establish materiality thresholds, and align on timeline milestones.

2

Data Collection

Our audit team requests ledger databases, invoices, bank balances, and internal control documents.

3

Testing & Synthesis

We run verification tests on transactions, cross-examine accounts, and compile discrepancy registers.

4

Final Reporting

We prepare the independent Auditor's Report, highlighting adjustments, key findings, and compliance validation.
